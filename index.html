Life is a Walk…

Life is a walk , Ya to tum apni zindagi mai upar jate ho ya to phir niche. Chahe tum upar jao ya phir niche tum apni zindagi mai ek chiz kabhi nahi rokoge “ki tum chalna nahi chhodoge.” Jab tumhe chot pohachti hai na , tab tum niche dekhte ho, niche dekhte dekhte tum usi raste par chalne lagte ho. Esliye jab bhi kabhi chot lage, hurt hojaye to ek cheez karr ki aage badh. Chahe jo koi insan jitni bhi chot dale , tuze aage badhna hai. Easy nahi hai par than le mann mai ki muze aage badhna hai. Agar tuze revenge leni hi hai to uska baap ban. Dekh upar wale ko niche wale ko chot pohchana easy hai , aur wo pohchayenge bhi. Niche wale ko dabana asan hai. Rojj lagne do chote kitni bhi ho, aisa lagega ki chod du sab, kamre mai band krke rota rahu bass, Usi time yadd lana “Nahi,Tumhe Aage Badhama Hai, Unse Bhi Aage Badhana Hai, Chahe Kitni Bhi Chote Lage Aage Badhunga Mai." Tu apna kam karaye ja, ek din tum jab usse bhi upar jaoge na , RAJA ban jayoge. Likh ke deta hoon. Tab wahi log jo tumhe chot pohchaye hua hai , ekdum se palatenge .Ye revenge vagere sab tumhari charanonki dool hogi , kuch importance nahi rahega isse. Tabhi wahi log pooja karne lagenge teri. Ekdum se sabka attitude change ho jayega , hasega tu , proud feel krega. Bass kam karaye ja!
